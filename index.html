A Letter To My To My Guiding Star 💌

Dear big sis, If I’m being honest, calling you just my “sister” has never really felt like enough… because you’ve been so much more in my life. You’ve been someone who checks up on me when you don’t have to, corrects me when I’m going the wrong way (even when I stubbornly think I’m right 😅), and somehow always manages to remind me to stay close to God and be better. And I can’t lie… sometimes you’ve also been my emergency sponsor when life and my wallet decide to fight me. Thank you for always coming through for me. I truly don’t take that for granted. But more than the help, it’s your heart that means the most to me. The way you care, advise me, encourage me, and still make time to look out for me shows the kind of person you are. Not everyone is lucky enough to have someone like you in their life… and I know I’m blessed that God gave me you as my sister. Even when I don’t say it often, please know that I see everything you do and I appreciate you more than words can explain. On your birthday, my prayer is that God blesses you with the same love, peace, and kindness that you’ve always shown me and the people around you. Happy Birthday, sis❤️ I’m grateful for you today and always.
Made with love 💖
